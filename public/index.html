The Process

Three steps to zero missed doses

Set up once. Let MediGuard handle the rest, every single day.

01
Step 01

Subscribe & Set Up

Pay your monthly plan and enter your name and WhatsApp number. Your profile is live in less than 3 minutes — no app, no password, no hassle.

02
Step 02

Build Your Schedule

Add each medicine, the dose, and the exact time you need your reminder. Morning insulin at 7 AM, blood pressure tablet at noon — you are in full control.

03
Step 03

Receive on WhatsApp

At exactly the right moment, MediGuard messages you directly on WhatsApp — personalised with your name and a warm reminder. No app, no login, no friction.
